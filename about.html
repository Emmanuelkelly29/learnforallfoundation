Who We Are

Our Foundation

Learn For All Foundation is a non-profit organization committed to ensuring that no child is left behind in the pursuit of education. We believe that education is the most powerful tool to break cycles of poverty and empower communities for generations.

Our foundation focuses on supporting vulnerable children, youths, and families in rural and underserved communities by providing resources, training, and opportunities that open doors to brighter futures.

Our Team

Our Story

Our Journey

The Beginning

The Learn For All Foundation was born out of a passion to see every child, regardless of background, given the chance to learn. We witnessed firsthand how many children in rural areas were denied education due to poverty, lack of resources, or community neglect.

We could not stand by and watch — so we started with small acts of support: donating books, sponsoring school fees, and providing mentorship. What began as a small step has now grown into a global mission to ensure that quality education reaches everyone who needs it most.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

"Our vision is to break the chains of poverty by ensuring access to quality education for every child. We dream of a world where no child's future is limited by circumstance, where vulnerable and rural communities have the same opportunities to learn, grow, and thrive. At Learn For All Foundation, we believe education is not a privilege — it is a right."

Our Mission

  • To provide scholarships and educational support to vulnerable children
  • To build partnerships that bring learning resources to rural communities
  • To empower families and communities through awareness and skills programs
  • To inspire a global movement where everyone plays a role in making education accessible for all

Our Core Values

Compassion

We approach every child and family with empathy, understanding their unique circumstances and challenges.

Equity

We believe every child deserves equal access to quality education, regardless of their background or circumstances.

Transparency

We maintain complete transparency in our operations, ensuring donors and partners know exactly how their contributions are used.

Community

We work closely with local communities, respecting their culture and involving them in our educational initiatives.
